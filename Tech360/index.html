We are working together and in collaboration with a number of international companies who have very high reputations for providing end to end services which include, among others, Supply, Survey, Planning, Designing and Execution of Telecom and IT services.

Who we are


Tech360 Limited is founded by a group of dynamic "Techno-entrepreneurs" with a common set of visions to be a leader in the Telecommunications space and provide innovative, optimized, cost effective and customized solutions to the clients locally and internationally to support its tremendous business expansion plan and also to strengthen its management and Technical capabilities. Tech360 Limited have an extensive know-how and proven track record of around 6 years in the Telecommunication industry.

Tech360 Limited can provide specialized consultancy services for customized requirements. Tech360 aims to deliver no-compromise fulfillment of our customers‟ expectations and to supply high-quality Products and Services for all communication networks and industries. Tech360 offers E2E Telecom solutions for a wide variety of customer networks.



Mission

Is to deliver specialist telecoms grade solutions that enable operators and governments to adapt quickly and effectively to changing and evolving telecoms networks. Tech360 also aim to respond to each customer’s requirements with an optimized solution, not only to satisfy these specific requirements, but also, to exceed the customer's expectations. The solution will ideally combine an individualized set of products and services, maximizing the benefit of investment in the new technology.

Vision

Is to be the preferred partner when solving complex telecom network challenges and continue to be an independent company serving the specialized needs of Telecom Network Operators.
