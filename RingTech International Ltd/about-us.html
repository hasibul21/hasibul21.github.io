About Us

RingTech International is a registered company which started its operations in the name of Global Telecom & Construction in 2004. Global Telecom & Construction turns into a Limited company and renamed as RingTech Limited in 2005. In 2010, RingTech International formed to carry out all telecom works of RingTech Limited with strengthen management and Technical capabilities and to support tremendous telecom business expansion in Bangladesh.


RingTech International is one of the leading Telecommunication servicing houses in Bangladesh providing end to end service for telecom network implementation. We are enlisted with all major telecom vendors and operators and working with almost all telecom network in Bangladesh. Earlier we have done RF, LOS and Civil survey in Nepal for NEPAL TELECOM. We have also completed GSM network implementation work at Bhutan for TASHI INFOCOM. We are authorized service provider of Soft Power Systems for their Telecom DC power systems. It provides, in the most professional manner, telecom services for a wide range of BTS, BSC, MSC, HLR, IN, Transmission as well as turnkey civil work. We are maintaining a roll of highly experience professionals specialized in diverse discipline and can offer the best team almost every time.


RingTech International is fully equipped with self owner testing tools like Site Master, Test Mobile, Power Meter, E1 error detector and other related equipment needed for testing and commissioning. RingTech International is well organized with experienced management and trained Engineer. It has 183 employees such as 37 Trained Engineers, 37 Expert Team Leaders and 91 well experienced Technicians. It has set up its business expansion plan to make minimum 40 expert team including Survey, BTS, MW & CN works.


RingTech International has achieved Excellent Improvement Award for year 2006 from Huawei Asia Pacific Region. RingTech International also achieved an award 2011 by Huawei Technologies Co. Limited for Excellent Service Provider in TI Field.
